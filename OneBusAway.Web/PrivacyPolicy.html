Privacy Policy
We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal information.

We do not track your searches or record your actions.

If you authorize OneBusAway to use your location, we will use your latitude and longitude to find nearby bus stops.

We do not store this information, it is sent anonymously to onebusaway.org to find nearby locations.
We're not interested in advertising to you.
We're not interested in selling your data.

We're just a group of volunteers who want to make public transportation easy and accessible to all of Seattle.
